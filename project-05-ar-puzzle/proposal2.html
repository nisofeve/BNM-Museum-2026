Rhythm of Nationality
Kong Kien Choong · 2004 · Media campuranMixed media
Merayakan kepelbagaian budaya Malaysia melalui jalur-jalur kain tradisional yang berwarna-warni. Setiap jalur mewakili kaum dan budaya berbeza, namun bersatu dalam satu komposisi harmoni. Celebrates Malaysia's cultural diversity through vibrant traditional fabric strips. Each strip represents a different community, united in one harmonious composition.

UNITY · PERPADUAN
Unity in Diversity Series 2
ArtJamila · 2017 · Dakwat atas kanvasInk on canvas
Menggambarkan perpaduan dalam kepelbagaian — setiap komuniti diwakili secara tersendiri, namun bertemu dalam ruang perkongsian bersama, simbol integrasi masyarakat Malaysia. Depicts unity in diversity — each community represented individually, yet meeting in a shared space, a symbol of Malaysian social integration.
