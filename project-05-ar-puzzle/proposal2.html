Rhythm of Nationality
Kong Kien Choong · 2004
Media campuran atas kanvas Mixed media on canvas
Karya ini merayakan kepelbagaian budaya Malaysia melalui jalur-jalur kain tradisional yang berwarna-warni. Setiap jalur mewakili kaum dan budaya yang berbeza, namun bersatu dalam satu komposisi yang harmoni — mencerminkan semangat kebangsaan Malaysia. This work celebrates Malaysia's rich cultural diversity through vibrant traditional fabric strips. Each strip represents a different community and culture, yet they unite in one harmonious composition — reflecting the spirit of the Malaysian nation.

UNITY · PERPADUAN
🎨 Lukisan 2 daripada 2Artwork 2 of 2
Unity in Diversity Series 2
ArtJamila · 2017
Dakwat di atas kanvas Ink on canvas
Karya ini menggambarkan perpaduan dalam kepelbagaian melalui bulatan-bulatan yang saling bertindih. Setiap bulatan mewakili komuniti yang berbeza, dan di mana mereka bertemu, lahirlah ruang perkongsian dan persefahaman — simbol integrasi masyarakat Malaysia. This artwork depicts unity in diversity through overlapping circles. Each circle represents a different community, and where they intersect, a space of sharing and understanding is born — a symbol of Malaysian social integration.
